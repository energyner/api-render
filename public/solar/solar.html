Product Description

Description

We have developed an initial version of this tool to facilitate the calculation of electricity production generated by solar collectors. Our goal is to progressively expand its structure, incorporating new properties and methods that enhance its functionality. This will improve its usefulness in decision‑making and in determining key parameters for evaluating the feasibility of using solar panels.

Usefulness

We are aware that this type of tool, by simplifying complex calculations related to energy use, can have a significant impact both locally and globally. Its usefulness goes beyond process automation, as it contributes to resource optimization, facilitates decision‑making, and promotes sustainable development in various communities.

Benefits

a) Global Impact

• Promotion of renewable energy: Provides access to accurate calculations, encouraging the adoption of solar energy in diverse communities.
• Resource optimization: Allows governments, industries, and households to evaluate the feasibility of solar panels and improve their efficiency.
• Contribution to the fight against climate change: By promoting the use of clean energy, it helps reduce dependence on fossil fuels and decrease carbon emissions.

b) Benefits for the Local Community

• Empowerment of users and businesses: Anyone, without advanced knowledge, can estimate their solar production and make better energy decisions.
• Investment optimization: Helps households and businesses calculate more accurately the return on investment in solar systems.
• Promotion of sustainable development: Cities can implement energy policies based on more accessible and concrete information.
